## 🔍 About This Project
I worked on this project to understand how people use Cyclistic bikes and what patterns exist in their usage.

The goal was to look at when people ride, where they ride the most, and how different users behave.

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- SQL  
- Tableau  

---

## 📊 What I Did
- Looked at how bike usage changes across different months  
- Compared subscribers and casual users  
- Identified locations with the highest number of trips  
- Analyzed popular routes and trip patterns  

---

## 💡 Key Insights
- Most rides happen between May and October (warmer months)  
- Subscribers make up the majority of users  
- Casual users ride less often but tend to take longer trips  
- Some areas have consistently higher demand than others  

---

## 📈 Why This Matters
These insights can help the business:
- Focus on high-demand locations  
- Improve marketing for casual users  
- Plan better for peak seasons
